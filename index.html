Point of view

The product surface is moving upstream.

Answer share will become a growth KPI.

If customers discover options inside AI answers, PMs need to measure whether the product appears, how it is framed, and what evidence the model trusts.

RAG quality is a product decision, not a model setting.

Chunking, retrieval, fallback, and evaluation choices shape user trust as much as interface copy or onboarding flow.

AI fluency should leave artifacts.

The strongest signal is not tool usage; it is a trail of prompts, specs, decisions, evals, and measurable improvements.
